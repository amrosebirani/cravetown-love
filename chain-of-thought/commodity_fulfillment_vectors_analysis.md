# Commodity Fulfillment Vectors Analysis

## Overview

This document analyzes all commodities in Cravetown and proposes fulfillment vectors - the ways citizens can consume/use commodities to satisfy their needs and cravings.

## Fulfillment Categories

Citizens have various needs that commodities can fulfill:

1. **Hunger/Nutrition** - Food consumption for sustenance
2. **Thirst** - Beverages and hydration
3. **Warmth** - Heating and warm clothing
4. **Shelter** - Housing and building materials
5. **Comfort** - Furniture and bedding
6. **Hygiene** - Soap, medicine, cleanliness
7. **Health** - Medicine, healing
8. **Clothing** - Garments for coverage and status
9. **Entertainment** - Books, art, music
10. **Status/Luxury** - High-end goods for prestige
11. **Light** - Candles, lamps
12. **Tools** - For work efficiency
13. **Spirituality** - Religious/ceremonial items
14. **Beauty** - Perfume, decorations
15. **Social** - Alcohol, gathering items

## FULFILLMENT PRIORITY MATRIX

For citizen satisfaction modeling, commodities should have priority weights:

### Essential Needs (Weight: High)
1. **Hunger** - bread, meal, rice dishes, dal, basic foods
2. **Thirst** - water, milk, juice
3. **Shelter** - housing materials
4. **Clothing** - simple_clothes, shoes
5. **Health** - medicine (when sick)

### Important Needs (Weight: Medium)
1. **Warmth** - winter_coat, firewood, warm food
2. **Hygiene** - soap
3. **Light** - candle, lamp_oil
4. **Comfort** - bed, chair
5. **Work tools** - appropriate tools for job

### Desire Needs (Weight: Low)
1. **Entertainment** - book, alcohol
2. **Status** - fine_clothes, jewelry
3. **Beauty** - perfume, mirror
4. **Luxury food** - pastries, biryani, sweets
5. **Social** - wine, beer

## COMMODITY QUALITY TIERS

Many commodities should have quality variants affecting satisfaction:

### Food Quality
- **Basic**: bread, roti, dal → Base satisfaction
- **Standard**: meal, paratha, pulao → 1.5x satisfaction
- **Fine**: biryani, pastries → 2x satisfaction
- **Luxury**: cake, specialty items → 3x satisfaction

### Clothing Quality
- **Basic**: simple_clothes → Meets need
- **Work**: work_clothes → +durability
- **Fine**: fine_clothes → +status
- **Luxury**: luxury_clothes → +status, prestige

### Furniture Quality
- Basic wood → Standard comfort
- Fine wood → +10% comfort
- Decorated → +20% comfort + status

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### Immediate Additions Needed

1. **Add Water as commodity** - Basic thirst fulfillment
   - Water well as building
   - Water collection/distribution

2. **Add Tea** - Important for Indian setting
   - Tea leaves commodity
   - Chai preparation recipe

3. **Add Lamp** - To use lamp_oil
   - Lamp as furniture item
   - Provides light radius

4. **Add Fish** - Complete protein sources
   - Fishery building (deferred)
   - Fish-based recipes

### Building Support Needed

For fulfillment to work, citizens need:
1. **Homes** with storage for personal goods
2. **Markets** to purchase goods
3. **Wells/Water sources** for water
4. **Taverns/Inns** for social drinking

### Craving System Integration

Map commodities to the 49 craving dimensions:
- Food commodities → hunger cravings
- Beverages → thirst cravings
- Clothing → warmth/coverage cravings
- Furniture → comfort cravings
- Luxury → status cravings
- etc.
